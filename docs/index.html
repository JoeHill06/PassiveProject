About GradeCalc

GradeCalc provides free, accurate grade calculators built specifically for UK university students. All calculations run entirely in your browser — your data is never sent to a server or stored anywhere.

Whether you are trying to work out your degree classification, figure out what marks you need on upcoming exams, or understand how the UK grading system works, our tools and guides are here to help.

Why Students Use GradeCalc

  • Accurate calculations: Our calculators use the same credit-weighted averaging methods that UK universities use.
  • Private: All calculations happen in your browser. We never collect or store your grades.
  • Free: Every tool and guide on GradeCalc is completely free to use, with no sign-up required.
  • Mobile-friendly: All tools work on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Disclaimer: GradeCalc is for educational purposes only and does not provide official academic advice. Grade boundaries, year weightings, and calculation methods vary between universities. Always check your own institution's regulations.
