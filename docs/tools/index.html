How Our Calculators Work

All GradeCalc tools use the standard credit-weighted average formula used by UK universities. Here's what makes them reliable:

  • Standard formulas: We use the same calculation methods described in university academic regulations across the UK
  • All weighting schemes: Every common year-weighting scheme is supported (40/60, 33/67, 25/75, 0/100, and more)
  • Client-side processing: Calculations run in your browser — your marks are never sent to a server or stored
  • Instant results: No loading, no sign-up, no email required

See our calculator methodology and review process for details on how we test formulas and handle corrections.

Important: These calculators are for guidance only. Grade boundaries, year weightings, and calculation methods vary between universities. Always check your institution's academic regulations. See our guide to degree classifications for more context.
