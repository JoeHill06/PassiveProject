Understanding the UK Grading System

The UK uses a unique degree classification system (First, 2:1, 2:2, Third) that differs from the GPA system used in many other countries. Your final classification depends on your module marks, credit values, and year weightings — and the details vary between universities. Our guides break down each part of this system so you know exactly where you stand.
